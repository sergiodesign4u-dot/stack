## 1. Product Objectives

**v1 to v2 change:** Objective 4 (trust/safety) promoted to Objective 1. Research found that no Ukrainian sport nutrition store communicates composition, testing, or origin in depth - and that this is the primary conversion barrier for the beginner segment (Benchmark Phase 4). Safety-first is not a nice-to-have; it is the product.

| # | Objective | Metric | Target (hypothesis) | Validation Status |
|---|-----------|--------|-------------------|-------------------|
| 1 | Build trust and reduce uncertainty for the safety-sensitive buyer | Product page trust signal engagement; return rate; bounce rate on product pages | <5% return rate, trust engagement on >60% sessions | CONFIRMED AND ELEVATED - benchmark showed all top performers lead with trust before price. UA market gap confirmed: no UA store does this well. Sources: thorne.com/nsf-certified-for-sport, bulk.com/uk/sports-nutrition/informed-sport |
| 2 | Build a trusted path from goal to product for non-expert buyers | Guided quiz completion rate; first-purchase conversion from quiz | 40% quiz completion, 20% quiz-to-purchase | CONFIRMED - goal selector is universal among top performers (Myprotein 6-goal selector, Huel quiz, Liki24 symptom nav). No UA sport nutrition store has an equivalent. Sources: us.myprotein.com/c/goal-selector/, huel.com/pages/choose-your-goal |
| 3 | Establish the coach/gym ordering channel as primary revenue driver | % of GMV from coach accounts; average coach order value | 40% of GMV from coach accounts within 6 months [?] | CONFIRMED AND STRENGTHENED - only belok.ua has a wholesale program (belok.ua/ua/opt/), and it is buried at a secondary URL with no front-of-site promotion. All other UA competitors have no coach channel. The differentiation gap is larger than hypothesized. |
| 4 | Drive repeat purchases and reduce catalog abandonment | 30-day repeat purchase rate; cart abandonment rate | 35% repeat rate, <60% abandonment [?] | CONFIRMED with nuance - subscription model is NOT viable for UA market MVP (see benchmark). Email/SMS reorder confirmed as the right approach. Sources: research/benchmark.md mechanism 3. |

## 2. Audience Segments

**v1 to v2 notes:**
- Coach segment: CONFIRMED as primary. Belok.ua's wholesale program (belok.ua/ua/opt/) confirms real market demand. Zero other UA sport nutrition competitors have any coach channel - the opportunity gap is validated.
- Beginner segment: CONFIRMED. Benchmark showed goal-to-product guidance is the #1 activation lever. Refinement: safety concern is more prominent than initially stated - it must appear before price.
- Regulars segment: CONFIRMED with correction. The auto-subscription path is NOT viable for UA (see benchmark). The correct retention mechanic is email/SMS reminders + one-tap reorder from history.

### Segment 1: Coaches, Gyms, Team Managers (Primary)

**Validation: CONFIRMED AND STRENGTHENED.** Belok.ua's wholesale program at belok.ua/ua/opt/ shows existing demand. No other UA competitor has any coach-specific tooling. Myprotein has trade accounts (myprotein.com footer), Thorne has professional tools (thorne.com community section) - both confirm this is a proven segment internationally. The gap in UA is large.

| Field | Detail |
|-------|--------|
| Age | 25-45 |
| Profile | Professional/semi-pro coaches, gym owners, team managers. Purchase for 5-30+ athletes. Know products well. Act as trust proxy for clients. Only belok.ua has any wholesale infrastructure for this segment - and it is a buried secondary-URL form, not a front-of-site channel. |
| Motivation | Serve athletes reliably. Maintain professional credibility. Save time on bulk ordering. |
| Pain | All existing UA stores are built for solo B2C. Ordering for multiple people with different needs is slow and error-prone. The only option (belok.ua wholesale) requires a form submission and callback - no self-service. |
| JTBD | When I am ordering supplements for my clients, I want to build multi-client orders in one session, so that I can serve my athletes reliably and maintain their trust in my professional recommendations. |
| Priority | PRIMARY - this channel brings multiple repeat customers per account, high AOV, and is completely underserved in UA. Sources: belok.ua/ua/opt/, myprotein.com trade accounts, thorne.com professional community |

### Segment 2: Non-Expert Beginners (Secondary)

**Validation: CONFIRMED. JTBD refined to foreground safety.** Benchmark showed the top conversion driver for new buyers is eliminating safety doubt before price. Liki24's symptom-based navigation (liki24.com "Симптоми" category) is the best local reference for how to meet a buyer who does not yet know product terminology. Huel's quiz (huel.com/pages/which-huel-survey) and Myprotein's 6-goal selector are the international gold standard.

| Field | Detail |
|-------|--------|
| Age | 18-35 |
| Profile | Starting a fitness journey or targeting a specific outcome (fat loss, muscle gain, energy, recovery). No or minimal sport nutrition knowledge. Arrives via coach referral, TikTok/Instagram, or goal-based search. |
| Motivation | Achieve a fitness goal safely. Get confident, clear direction. Importantly: not appear foolish, not make a dangerous mistake. |
| Pain | Enormous catalog, unfamiliar terminology, fear of side effects or wrong products. No single trusted source. Cannot evaluate "100% original" claims. |
| JTBD | When I want to improve my body or performance but feel overwhelmed and unsafe with the catalog, I want to answer a few plain questions and get a clear, verified product set for my goal, so that I can start confidently without becoming an expert first. [Updated: "unsafe" added based on benchmark trust findings] |
| Priority | SECONDARY - high volume acquisition path; requires more guidance investment but trust systems built for this segment serve all segments |

### Segment 3: Experienced Regulars ("Always in Stock") (Supporting / Later)

**Validation: CONFIRMED with subscription correction.** The auto-subscription model used by Thorne, Bulk, and AG1 is NOT viable for UA market at MVP stage (benchmark finding). The correct approach is: one-tap reorder from order history + email/SMS reminders. Belok.ua's cumulative discount (5% after 10,000 UAH / 10% after 50,000 UAH, belok.ua/ua/sistema-skidok/) is a relevant local reference for non-subscription loyalty.

| Field | Detail |
|-------|--------|
| Age | 22-40 |
| Profile | Experienced buyers with established supplement routines. Know exactly what they want. Time-poor. Loyal to a store they trust and will stay if it is reliable. |
| Motivation | Never run out of staples. Minimize decision effort for repeat purchases. |
| Pain | Re-navigating the catalog every time. Out-of-stock surprises. No reminder or auto-reorder system. Having to re-enter address/payment. |
| JTBD | When I am running low on my staple products, I want to reorder in one or two taps, so that I never run out and don't waste mental energy on repeat decisions. |
| Priority | SUPPORTING/LATER - valuable for LTV and retention; reorder as free MVP convenience via email/SMS + order history. Subscription tier = open question for Phase 2. Sources: belok.ua/ua/sistema-skidok/ |

## 3. AIDA per Segment

**v1 to v2 notes:**
- Coaches: CONFIRMED. Added Telegram as primary outreach channel - Ukrainian fitness professionals are heavily active on Telegram.
- Beginners: REFINED. Added concern-based secondary path alongside goal-based primary path, based on Liki24's symptom navigation benchmark finding.
- Regulars: CONFIRMED with subscription correction. Email/SMS confirmed as correct channel; subscription removed from Desire stage.

### Segment 1: Coaches / Gyms

| AIDA Stage | Channel | Message | Mechanic |
|------------|---------|---------|----------|
| Attention | Instagram/TikTok for coaches, Telegram fitness communities, word-of-mouth at gyms, direct outreach to gym owners | "Ordering for your athletes? There is a better way." | Short-form video showing the pain of current ordering (belok.ua wholesale form = callback required) vs. Stack's self-service multi-client cart |
| Interest | Dedicated "For Coaches" landing page, coach section in navigation | "One session. All your clients. Bulk pricing, saved profiles, fast reorder." | Coach account demo video, testimonial from a gym owner, side-by-side comparison vs. current process |
| Desire | Coach account signup flow, onboarding | "See how much time you save on your first order." | Free first month of coach account features; personalized onboarding (early stage); pricing transparency for bulk orders |
| Action | Coach registration, first multi-client order | "Set up your team in 5 minutes." | Streamlined onboarding with client profile import; clear first-order CTA; minimal steps to first purchase |

### Segment 2: Non-Expert Beginners

| AIDA Stage | Channel | Message | Mechanic |
|------------|---------|---------|----------|
| Attention | Social (TikTok/Instagram), goal-based search ("що приймати для схуднення"), referral from coach | "Not sure what to take? Start here." | Goal-based AND concern-based homepage entry points; search-optimized goal landing pages |
| Interest | Goal quiz + concern-based secondary path | "Three questions. A safe, verified product plan for your goal." | Interactive goal quiz with immediate results (primary); concern-based path for those who can't identify a fitness goal (secondary, modeled on Liki24 symptom nav) |
| Desire | Quiz results page, product pages | "This is exactly what you need - and here is why it is safe." | Composition breakdown above the fold; dosage guide; origin/certification badge; "verified by Stack" quality signal; "approved by coach" social proof if referred |
| Action | Add to cart from quiz results, simplified checkout | "Your starter pack is ready." | Pre-built goal bundle add-to-cart from quiz results; one-click guest checkout option |

### Segment 3: Regulars

| AIDA Stage | Channel | Message | Mechanic |
|------------|---------|---------|----------|
| Attention | Email/SMS consumption reminder, account dashboard notification | "You are probably running low on [product]." | Consumption-estimated email/SMS sent 5-7 days before likely stockout; opt-in at checkout |
| Interest | Account "My Staples" saved list, order history | "Reorder everything in one tap." | Saved product list with stock status shown; one-tap repeat order from history |
| Desire | Order history page | "Same as last time. Same price. In stock." | Stock availability on saved items; price consistency; no re-navigation required |
| Action | One-tap reorder | "Done." | Minimal confirmation flow; saved address and payment method; no new checkout steps |

## 4. AIDA to AARRR Mapping

**v1 to v2 note:** Coach channel now has its own distinct funnel path annotated.

| AIDA Stage | AARRR Stage | Notes | Coach channel path |
|------------|-------------|-------|--------------------|
| Attention | Acquisition | Paid social, SEO, coach outreach, referral | Direct outreach via Telegram/Instagram; gym community word-of-mouth |
| Interest | Activation | Goal quiz, product guidance, coach account demo | Coach landing page; multi-client cart demo; account creation |
| Desire | Activation / Revenue | Product page trust signals, bundle builder, coach tools | Bulk pricing preview; client profile builder; first-order flow |
| Action | Revenue | First purchase | First multi-client order placed |
| (Post-purchase) | Retention | Email/SMS reorder reminders, account history, My Staples | Coach account reorder; client list management; stock alerts |
| (Sharing) | Referral | Coach spreading to athletes, social sharing | Coach referral link - each athlete arrives pre-trusted |
